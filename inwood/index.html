Why inWood?

inWood is a comprehensive, interactive resource that teaches college students the fundamentals of wood anatomy.

Based on an accredited college course

inWood is originally a resource tool designed for the use of college students at Iowa State taking BIOL 454, Plant Anatomy. This class is taught under the instruction or supervision of Dr. Lynn Clark, Ph.D. In fact, the designer and developer of this website, Ace Lee, took the course herself!

Engaging quizzes

inWood has 7 engaging quizzes for the 7 topics that covers the foundations of Wood Anatomy. All questions are randomized and displays the learner's scores. These quizzes are also equipped with encouraging, but sarcastic humor!
