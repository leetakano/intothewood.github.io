Why Into the Wood?

Into the Wood is a comprehensive, interactive resource for anyone who wishes to learn the foundations of wood anatomy.

Interactive quizzes

Into the Wood has 7 interactive quizzes that cover all 7 topics that make up the foundations of wood anatomy:

  1. Wood and the importance of wood anatomy
  2. Gymnosperms
  3. Angiosperms
  4. Secondary Growth
  5. Bark
  6. Vascular Cambium
  7. Principle Planes

Based on an accredited college course

Check out our glossary

Struggling with unfamiliar words and terminology? Find your answer using our glossary. We designed our glossary in the form of flashcards to promote active recall in your learning. Each flashcard comes with a photograph, a drawing, as well as a description.
