About Me

I recently graduated with a Bachelor's in Biological Pre-medical Illustration at Iowa State University. But in my last semester, I came to realize that I enjoyed doing tech projects more than fine arts projects.

The tech projects I worked on involved data visualization using Processing3, UI/UX design using Adobe XD, and web development using HTML5, CSS, and JavaScript. These projects also played an important role in winning 2 awards. Both came with cash prizes.

The impact these projects made on me and my peers inspired me to embark on a journey to become an IT professional

Currently, I’m attending a 12-week bootcamp at Per Scholas on Mainframe Engineering. There, I’m learning the fundamentals of TSO/ISPF, JCL, COBOL, DB2, CICS, and more.
